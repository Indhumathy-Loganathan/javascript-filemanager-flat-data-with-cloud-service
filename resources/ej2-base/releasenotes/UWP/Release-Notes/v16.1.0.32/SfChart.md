## SfChart

### Bug Fixes
{:#sfchart-bug-fixes}

* \#199089 - The [`FastLineSeries`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/uwp/Syncfusion.SfChart.UWP~Syncfusion.UI.Xaml.Charts.FastLineSeries.html) will not be disappeared while zooming the chart with [`CategoryAxis`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/uwp/Syncfusion.SfChart.UWP~Syncfusion.UI.Xaml.Charts.CategoryAxis.html).
 
* \#200399 - [`Trendlines`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/uwp/Syncfusion.SfChart.UWP~Syncfusion.UI.Xaml.Charts.CartesianSeries~Trendlines.html) will be loaded properly for two data points.
 
* \#136320 - The ArgumentOutOfRangeException will no longer be thrown when call the [`SuspendSeriesNotification`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/uwp/Syncfusion.SfChart.UWP~Syncfusion.UI.Xaml.Charts.ChartBase~SuspendSeriesNotification.html) and [`ResumeSeriesNotification`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/uwp/Syncfusion.SfChart.UWP~Syncfusion.UI.Xaml.Charts.ChartBase~ResumeSeriesNotification.html) methods dynamically.
 
* \#201329 - The NullReferenceException will no longer thrown when set the [`SfChart.Series`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/uwp/Syncfusion.SfChart.UWP~Syncfusion.UI.Xaml.Charts.SfChart~Series.html) property to null.
