## SfChart

### Bug Fixes
{:#sfchart-bug-fixes}


*  \#184969 – Now, SideBySide(ColumnSeries, BarSeries etc.,) series will be rendered properly while having one data with non-SBS series.

*  \#186061 – Now, [`EnableClipping`](https://help.syncfusion.com/uwp/sfchart/annotations#text-annotation) property will work properly for [`TextAnnotation`](https://help.syncfusion.com/uwp/sfchart/annotations#text-annotation).

*  \#186076 – Now, [`GridLines`](https://help.syncfusion.com/uwp/sfchart/axis#gridlines-and-ticklines) will be rendered properly, while changing its visibility dynamically.

*  \#185138 – Now, SideBySide series will be rendered properly when binding Line Series X-Axis to SBS Series X-Axis. 

*  \#132272 – Now, [`Adornment`](https://help.syncfusion.com/uwp/sfchart/adornments) labels will be updated properly when changing  [`itemsSource`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/uwp/Syncfusion.SfChart.UWP~Syncfusion.UI.Xaml.Charts.ChartSeriesBase~ItemsSource.html) dynamically for [`RangeSeries`](https://help.syncfusion.com/uwp/sfchart/series#range-series).
*  \#149882, #156728 – Now, [`SfChart`](https://help.syncfusion.com/uwp/sfchart/getting-started) [`Zooming`](https://help.syncfusion.com/uwp/sfchart/interactive-features#zoom-and-pan) and [`panning`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/uwp/Syncfusion.SfChart.UWP~Syncfusion.UI.Xaml.Charts.ChartZoomPanBehavior~EnablePanning.html) performance was improved in UWP mobile.
