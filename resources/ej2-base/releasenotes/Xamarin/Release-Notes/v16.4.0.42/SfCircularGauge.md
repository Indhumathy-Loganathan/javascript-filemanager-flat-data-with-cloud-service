## SfCircularGauge

### Bug fixes
{:#sfcirculargauge-bug-fixes}

* \# 139647 -  The gradient colors for [`Ranges`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/xamarin/Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms~Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms.Scale~Ranges.html) will be applied properly even on dynamic change of range's [`StartValue`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/xamarin/Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms~Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms.Range~StartValue.html) and [`EndValue`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/xamarin/Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms~Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms.Range~EndValue.html).

* \# 140149 - The [`Thickness`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/xamarin/Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms~Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms.NeedlePointer~Thickness.html) of [`NeedlePointer`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/xamarin/Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms~Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms.Range~EndValue.html) will no longer change on page navigation in Xamarin.Forms for iOS.


### Features
{:#sfcirculargauge-features}

*  \# 137683, #212950 -	Support has been provided to align the gauge to the center based on the [`StartAngle`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/xamarin/Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms~Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms.Scale~StartAngle.html) and [`SweepAngle`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/xamarin/Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms~Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms.Scale~SweepAngle.html).

*  \#204272 -  Support has been provided for custom labels in circular gauge [`Scales`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/xamarin/Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms~Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms.Scale.html).

*  Support has been provided to align the [`Header`](https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/cref_files/xamarin/Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms~Syncfusion.SfGauge.XForms.Header.html) in both vertical and horizontal directions with respect to the specified position.
